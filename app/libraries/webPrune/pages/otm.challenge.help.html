General

A challenge is a way to time your training or to be able to compete against others.

Editing

You create a challenge by adding "gates" to the the trail. For a run to be ok all gates have to be crossed in the correct order. That is, it is not possible to ride a challenge in the reverse direction. You can add gates by right clicking the map and choose add, and you can remove gates by right clicking a gate and choose delete.

After a gate has been placed on the map, the individual parts of the gates can be moved by draging it. The entire gate can be moved by pressing shift while dragging.

Auto Add Gates

You can let the software add gates automaticly. The gates will be added evenly spaced to the map.

Auto Tune

The Auto Tune feature will try to move and rotate the gate to best fit the trail.
